Day & Time

As opposed to the first couple of graphs, the "Day & Time" graph presents data on each individual candidate's engagement metrics rather than comparing candidates to each other. Here users may try to see whether metrics differ across hours of the day in which tweets were published or even across days of the week.

Notes:

  • Another metric, "Count", was added for this visualization in addition to "Retweets" and "Favorites". Count measures the total number of tweets the account published for that hour or day of the week for the date range provided, whereas Retweets/Favorites measure the average engagement metric received from twitter users for those categories.
